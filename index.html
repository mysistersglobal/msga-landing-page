A Letter From Our Founder

Welcome to the Alliance

My name is Romona Armon, and I am the Founder & CEO of My Sisters Global Alliance.

For many years, I have had the privilege of walking alongside women and girls during some of life's most difficult and most meaningful moments. I've learned that sometimes what changes a life isn't just a program—it's knowing that someone truly cares and is willing to show up.

That belief is what inspired My Sisters Global Alliance.

Our mission is to educate, equip, and empower women and girls by providing leadership development, mentorship, practical resources, community support, advocacy, and opportunities that create lasting impact.

My vision is simple: to build a global alliance where women know they are never alone. No matter where they are in the world, they have a community that believes in them, stands beside them, and is committed to helping them thrive.

Thank you for joining us as we begin this journey together.

Welcome to the Alliance.

Romona Armon

Founder & CEO, My Sisters Global Alliance
